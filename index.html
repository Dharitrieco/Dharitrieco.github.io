Wrap the Future in Green with Sustainable Alternatives

Our Mission

Our packaging is for the conscious creator. Every Dharitri product is a step toward cleaner skies and greener lands. From food trays to paper bags, we ensure everything you carry reflects care—for the environment, and for future generations.

How We Make It

We upcycle agricultural by-products like sugarcane bagasse and rice husk—often burned by farmers—into compostable packaging. This not only helps reduce air pollution and waste but also replaces plastic with purpose.
